Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my reading progress?

Visit the Reading Tracker page and enter your book details including total pages and pages read. The tracker calculates your progress percentage and estimated finish date.

Can I save my favorite books?

Yes! On the Recommender page, you can save book recommendations to your reading list. Your reading list is saved locally in your browser.
